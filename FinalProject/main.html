Rules:

Starting the game

To start the game, click the "ROLL" button. This will roll the dice for both the player and the computer. The game is played in 3 rounds.

Playing the game

In each round, the player and the computer will roll the dice.

After each roll the score will be calculated based off of the following rules:

  • Zero Score: If either of the dice shows a 1, the score of that round is zero.
    For example a roll of 6 and 1 scores 0
  • Double Score: If both dice show the same number, the score is double the total of te dice. However this excludes 1 as it will result in a score of 0.
    For example a roll of 5 and 5 will score 20
  • Standard Score: For any other dice combination, the score is the some of the dice
    For example a roll of 3 and 2 scores 7

Repeat the following process for the 3 rounds of the game

Determining the Winner

After each round, the player and the computer will have their scores updated. The player with the highest score after 3 rounds is the winner.
